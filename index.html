CHIN 101-08, Fall 2024

Course Description:

This course will provide students with an introduction to Mandarin Chinese. Through rigorous practice, the students will learn sounds, vocabulary, and grammatical structures used in everyday life situations. This course is designed to help students to develop basic oral skills so that they can carry out simple conversations on everyday topics. It also helps students to advance their literacy skills in Chinese through a systematic introduction to the Chinese writing system. By the end of the semester students will gain a basic understanding of Chinese language and culture.
Students may earn credit for the first semester foreign language course (level 10100) without completing the second course, only once.
